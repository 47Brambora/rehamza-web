BrainCoach

When rehabilitation becomes fun

Unique software for memory support and fine motor skills that combines medical expertise with enjoyment.
Ideal for brain training and rehabilitation after injuries or illnesses.

We are creators of innovative solutions for neurorehabilitation. Our approach combines the latest scientific research with modern technology to provide patients with the best possible options for recovery and improvement of their cognitive and motor functions.

How it works

Our system is designed with maximum simplicity and efficiency. Here are the steps to begin your journey to better health.

Diagnostics

We start with a detailed assessment of your cognitive functions using modern tests and examinations.

Personal program

We create a program tailored specifically to your needs and rehabilitation goals.

Regular exercises

You exercise at home or online under our expert supervision with regular check-ups.

Every exercise is specially designed to be both effective and enjoyable. Our software automatically adapts the difficulty level based on your progress.
